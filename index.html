The Problem

Today’s data rooms are document vaults.

Investors download files. Analysts read decks. Insights get buried in private notes.

The intelligence never compounds.

The Model

ZetaRooms is built on three primitives.

ZetaRooms

Company knowledge spaces.

Founders publish their narrative — pitch decks, financials, product docs, market research — in a structured, AI-readable workspace.

ZetaBases

Investor knowledge workspaces.

Every insight, analysis, and saved Zed conversation accumulates into your firm’s private intelligence archive.

Zed

Your personal AI analyst.

Zed reads ZetaRooms, learns from your ZetaBase, and helps you evaluate companies with the full context of your investment thinking.
